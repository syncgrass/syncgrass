Geodetic Engineering Services

SyncGrass provides precision surveying, geodetic control networks, GNSS solutions, and coordinate system establishment for infrastructure and land development projects. Our geodetic engineers ensure accurate, reliable spatial reference frameworks for your critical projects.

Our services include:

  • Geodetic Control Network Design and Establishment
  • GNSS/GPS Surveying and RTK Solutions
  • Coordinate System and Datum Transformations
  • Precision Leveling and Height Determination
  • Cadastral and Engineering Surveys
  • Deformation Monitoring and Subsidence Analysis
  • Geodetic Data Processing and Quality Assurance
